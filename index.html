Welcome to the documentation for DLminiboxThis library provides functions, modules and optimizers built on top of an automatic differentiation system allowing you to build powerful deep learning AI models. Please have a look at the tutorial to understand the theoretical foundation of this system, which uses backpropagation at its core.
